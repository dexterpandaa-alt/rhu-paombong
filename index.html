General outpatient consultations for illnesses, injuries, and health concerns conducted by licensed physicians and health workers.
Weekdays · Free

Comprehensive prenatal check-ups, nutrition counseling, and safe delivery services to ensure healthy mothers and babies.
By Schedule
💉
Immunization Program

Tues & Thurs

Confidential

Mon–Fri

All Ages

About the RHU

Serving Paombong with Compassion & Care

The Rural Health Unit of Paombong, Bulacan is a government-operated primary healthcare facility under the Department of Health (DOH) and the Local Government Unit of Paombong. We are committed to providing equitable, affordable, and quality healthcare to all residents.

🏛️
Government-Run Facility
Operated by the LGU of Paombong under the supervision of the Bulacan Provincial Health Office and DOH Region III.
📊
Digital Health Records
We use a modern digital patient records system to ensure accurate, secure, and efficient management of all health information.
🤝
Community-Centered Care
Our Barangay Health Workers (BHWs) actively serve all 18 barangays through outreach programs and home visits.
